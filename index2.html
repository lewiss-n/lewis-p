Makosa Imefanyika Campaign

A reactive, culturally rooted campaign that turned an internal design error into KFC Kenya's most-celebrated social media moment. By leaning into Nairobi office culture, Sheng, and self-aware humour, the brand transformed a mistake into a movement — and in doing so, defined a new creative direction for the entire account.

What made it work

The campaign succeeded because it was honest. Rather than burying the mistake, Lewis directed a response that acknowledged it in the brand's own voice — playful, Kenyan, and utterly relatable. The post spread organically through LinkedIn and Twitter, drawing commentary from marketing professionals across East Africa who cited it as a masterclass in authentic brand behaviour.
